01

Introduction

Saga AI Research Labs ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile applications, AI services, and other products (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

02

Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, password
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, bio, preferences
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number (if provided)
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely by third-party payment processors)
  • Communication Data: Messages, support requests, feedback

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect information about how you use our Services:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
  • Performance Data: Error logs, crash reports, response times
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (not precise location)

2.3 AI Interaction Data

When you use our AI services, we may collect:

  • Input Data: Images and text prompts you explicitly submit for AI video generation, which are transmitted to our third-party processing partner kie.ai
  • Output Data: AI-generated responses and content
  • Interaction Patterns: How you interact with AI-generated content
  • Feedback Data: Ratings, corrections, and feedback on AI responses

2.4 Trading and Financial Data

For our trading and financial services:

  • Portfolio Data: Holdings, balances, transaction history
  • Trading Preferences: Risk tolerance, investment goals, strategies
  • Market Data: Price feeds, market analysis, trading signals
  • Performance Metrics: Returns, profit/loss, success rates

Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their commercial purposes.

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • AI Video Generation (kie.ai) — processes images and text prompts submitted by users for video generation

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

05

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Data Minimization: We collect only necessary data
  • Secure Infrastructure: Enterprise-grade cloud security
  • Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training

06

Data Retention

We retain your information for different periods based on the type of data:

  • Account Data: Until account deletion + 30 days
  • Usage Analytics: 24 months (anonymized after 12 months)
  • AI Training Data: Up to 5 years (if you opt in) or 30 days (if you opt out)
  • Financial Data: 7 years (regulatory requirement)
  • Support Communications: 3 years
  • Legal/Compliance Data: As required by law

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

7.1 Universal Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7.2 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Automated Decision-Making: Opt out of automated profiling
  • Data Protection Officer: Contact our DPO with concerns

7.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Know: What personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out: Opt out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices
